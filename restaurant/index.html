Have you ever dreamed of eating frozen pizza in an actual palace where actual royalty held court? Dream no more.

At Cory's Pizza Palace, located in the beautiful, poverty-stricken, and secluded city of Salem, Ohio, you can indulge your desire to eat pizza in a true palace. Resident billionare Cory Bandrowsky has paid a ludicrous sum of money to deliver an intact serbian palace, the former of home of Pizza Lord Sau-Sage, to Salem. The palace has been staffed with adequate, yet underwhelming, servers who are prepared to quickly throw your choice of frozen pizza into an oven which has not been pre-heated, at your whim!

So come on down to the Pizza Palace, where the pizza is okay and the palace is pristine!
